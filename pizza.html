The dough recipe below should make enough for 4 pizzas.

  • 320g bread or 00 flour
  • 2g salt
  • 18g oil
  • 5g dried yeast
  • 208g water
  • 5g sugar
  1. Mix the water, dried yeast, and sugar. Leave to stand for at least 10 minutes.
  2. Combine the bread and salt in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add the oil to the water/yeast mix, briefly stir and pour into the mixing bowl.
  4. Thoroughly combine all the ingredients and briefly knead.
  5. Cover bowl with cling film and place in fridge for 24-72 hours.
  6. Remove from fridge and divide into 4 dough balls. Dust with flour and cover for 1-3 hours.
  7. The dough is ready to use.
